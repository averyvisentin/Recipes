Meatloaf Balls

"Meatloaf" Balls have been a staple of my childhood and adulthood. they're quick and easy to make, great with a variety of sides and I love them and I'm hoping you will too after you try them

You will need the following

  • One Egg
  • One Box of Stovetop stuffing
  • Ketchup
  • Barbeque Sauce
  • One pound of ground beef
  • A large bowl for mixing
  • Optional A clove of garlic

LETS GET STARTED

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 350 degrees
  2. Take your bowl and empty the stuffing mix, read the back and add half the water it requires
  3. Mix the stuffing and then add your ground beef, mix it all with (clean!) hands or spoon
  4. Crack your egg and add to beef and stuffing, mix
  5. Add a generous squirt of barbeque sauce and mix again
  6. If using garlic, mince and add it to mix, mix
  7. Spoon your meat into the muffin tray, filling the cups entirely
  8. Give each cup a little squirt of ketchup and spread with a spoon
  9. Throw it in the oven and cook for 30 minutes
  10. Enjoy! Eat it with your favorite side or by themselves!
